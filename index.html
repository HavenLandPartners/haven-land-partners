About Haven Land Partners

Haven Land Partners was founded by Ridge and Daniel, two entrepreneurs who understand how overwhelming owning unwanted land can feel. After years of building businesses together, they realized there was a better way to help landowners move forward — with honesty, transparency, and respect.

They created Haven Land Partners to give landowners peace of mind: a simple process, a fair cash offer, and a partner you can trust to take care of the details. Whether you inherited land you don’t need, are behind on property taxes, or simply want to move on, Ridge and Daniel are here to make it stress-free.

Their mission is in the name — to be a haven for landowners. With clear communication, no hidden fees, and a reputation for treating people like neighbors, Haven Land Partners is built on values that matter.
